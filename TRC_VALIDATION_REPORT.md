## How to read this

1. **Manual TRC 4 → suggested 3** is the biggest group. The document reserves TRC 4 for
   devices with proven >50% downtime, >50% cumulative cost, or no manufacturer support.
   With an empty maintenance log the data cannot prove that yet. If these devices truly
   fail often, logging their repairs will pull the suggestion down to 4 automatically.
   Alternatively, set their support status (e.g. Obsolete) in the log or config.
2. **Manual TRC 5 → suggested 4**: by design — TRC 5 (unsafe/no parts) is never auto-suggested.
3. **Next step:** confirm or correct support status per model, start logging maintenance,
   and mark devices that hold a quality certificate in the new شهادة الجودة column.
